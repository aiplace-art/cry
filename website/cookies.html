🍪 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and provide information to website owners.

Cookies contain information that is transferred to your device's hard drive. They help us recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions on our platform.

Key Points About Cookies:

  • Cookies do not contain viruses or malware
  • They cannot access other data on your device
  • They help improve your browsing experience
  • You can control or delete cookies at any time
  • Some cookies are essential for website functionality

Types of Technologies We Use

In addition to cookies, we use similar tracking technologies including:

  • Web Beacons (Pixels): Small graphic images that allow us to monitor user activity and site traffic patterns
  • Local Storage: HTML5 local storage that allows websites to store data locally in your browser
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage that is cleared when you close your browser
  • SDKs: Software development kits that collect information about how you interact with our mobile applications

📊 3. Types of Cookies We Use

By Duration

Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They help us maintain your session as you navigate through our platform.

Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They help us remember your preferences and settings across multiple visits.

By Category

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for our platform to function properly. Without these cookies, services like wallet connection, account authentication, and basic navigation cannot be provided. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

These cookies cannot be disabled as they are critical for platform security and functionality.

Analytics and Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our platform by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This data helps us improve our services, identify technical issues, and optimize user experience.

You can opt-out of analytics cookies through our cookie consent manager.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences, language settings, and customizations you've made to the platform interface.

You can disable these cookies, but some features may not work as intended.

Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests. They also help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns and limit the number of times you see an advertisement.

You can opt-out of marketing cookies at any time.

🛠️ 6. How to Control Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences through several methods:

Cookie Consent Manager

When you first visit HypeAI, you'll see a cookie banner that allows you to accept or customize your cookie preferences. You can change these preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Below are instructions for popular browsers:

🌐 Google Chrome

  1. Click the three dots menu (⋮) in the top right
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click "Privacy and security"
  4. Select "Cookies and other site data"
  5. Choose your preferred cookie settings

🦊 Mozilla Firefox

  1. Click the menu button (≡) in the top right
  2. Select "Settings" or "Options"
  3. Click "Privacy & Security"
  4. Under "Cookies and Site Data", adjust settings
  5. Click "Manage Data" to remove specific cookies

🧭 Safari

  1. Click "Safari" in the menu bar
  2. Select "Preferences"
  3. Click the "Privacy" tab
  4. Adjust cookie and tracking settings
  5. Click "Manage Website Data" to remove cookies

🔷 Microsoft Edge

  1. Click the three dots menu (…) in the top right
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click "Cookies and site permissions"
  4. Select "Cookies and site data"
  5. Choose your cookie preferences

🎭 Brave Browser

  1. Click the menu button in the top right
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click "Shields"
  4. Adjust "Cookies" blocking settings
  5. Manage site-specific permissions

📱 Mobile Safari (iOS)

  1. Open iPhone/iPad Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap "Safari"
  3. Under "Privacy & Security", adjust settings
  4. Toggle "Block All Cookies" or "Prevent Cross-Site Tracking"

⚠️ Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable all cookies, some features of HypeAI may not function properly. Specifically:

  • You may not be able to connect your wallet or maintain login sessions
  • Your preferences and settings will not be saved
  • Some security features may not work correctly
  • You may need to re-enter information on each visit

Do Not Track (DNT) Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not want to be tracked. Currently, there is no industry standard for how websites should respond to DNT signals. HypeAI honors DNT signals for optional cookies (analytics, marketing) but continues to use strictly necessary cookies for platform functionality and security.

⚖️ 7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding cookies and personal data under regulations like GDPR (Europe) and CCPA (California).

GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request information about cookies and data we've collected
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw cookie consent at any time

CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of data collection and sharing practices
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the "sale" of personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

✅ HypeAI Does NOT Sell Personal Data

We want to be clear: HypeAI does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. While we use third-party analytics and advertising services, this does not constitute a "sale" under most privacy regulations.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in the Contact section below. We will respond to verified requests within:

  • 30 days for GDPR requests
  • 45 days for CCPA requests (with possible 45-day extension)

Opt-Out Options

You can opt-out of specific tracking technologies:

  • Google Analytics: Install Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Interest-Based Advertising: Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • Network Advertising Initiative: NAI Opt-Out
