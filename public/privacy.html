By using HypeAI's platform, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use our services.

Key Principle: We believe in data minimization. We only collect data that is necessary to provide our AI-powered services and improve your trading experience.

This policy applies to:

5. Blockchain and Wallet Data

5.1 Wallet Connection

When you connect your crypto wallet to HypeAI:

  • We access your public wallet address to identify your account
  • We read your token balances from the blockchain (public data)
  • We request transaction signatures for authentication
  • We DO NOT have access to your private keys or seed phrases
  • You remain in full custody of your crypto assets

5.2 On-Chain Transactions

All blockchain transactions are public and permanent:

Public Data: Anyone can view your wallet address, token balances, transaction history, and smart contract interactions on blockchain explorers (Etherscan, PolygonScan, etc.).
  • Token transfers to/from HypeAI contracts
  • Staking deposits and withdrawals
  • Reward claims and distributions
  • DAO governance votes
  • DEX trades and liquidity provisions

5.3 Smart Contract Interactions

When you interact with HypeAI smart contracts:

  • We request your approval for specific contract functions
  • You sign transactions in your wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.)
  • Gas fees are paid by you to the blockchain network
  • Transactions are irreversible once confirmed
  • We do not control the blockchain and cannot reverse transactions

5.4 Wallet Privacy Best Practices

Recommendations:
  • Use a separate wallet for DeFi interactions (not your main wallet)
  • Consider using privacy-focused wallets or mixers if anonymity is important
  • Be aware that blockchain data is permanent and public
  • Never share your private keys or seed phrases with anyone
  • Enable two-factor authentication on your wallet if available

7. Data Security

7.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit (HTTPS)
  • Database encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Password hashing: Bcrypt hashing with salt (never stored in plain text)
  • Access controls: Role-based access, principle of least privilege
  • Firewall protection: Network-level security and intrusion detection
  • DDoS protection: Cloudflare and AWS Shield
  • Regular audits: Third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • Bug bounty program: Rewards for responsible disclosure

7.2 Smart Contract Security

  • Audited by reputable firms (CertiK, Quantstamp, etc.)
  • ReentrancyGuard to prevent reentrancy attacks
  • SafeMath for overflow protection
  • Emergency pause functionality
  • Multi-signature wallets for treasury management
  • Time-locked upgrades and governance

7.3 Account Security

We provide tools to secure your account:

  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): TOTP-based 2FA (Google Authenticator, Authy)
  • Email verification: Confirm email address during registration
  • Login alerts: Notifications for new device logins
  • Session management: Automatic logout after inactivity
  • Password requirements: Strong password enforcement

7.4 Data Breach Protocol

In the unlikely event of a data breach:

  • We will notify affected users within 72 hours (GDPR requirement)
  • We will report the breach to relevant authorities
  • We will provide details about the breach and affected data
  • We will offer remediation steps (password reset, 2FA setup)
  • We will publish a public incident report (if required)

7.5 Your Responsibilities

Protect Your Account:
  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Never share your password, private keys, or seed phrases
  • Be cautious of phishing emails and fake websites
  • Use official HypeAI domains (hypeai.io) only
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

11. CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

You can request disclosure of:

  • Business purposes for collecting data
  • Categories of third parties we share data with
  • Specific pieces of personal information we hold

11.2 Right to Delete

You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal exceptions (e.g., transaction records for tax compliance).

11.3 Right to Opt-Out of Sale

We DO NOT sell your personal information. HypeAI has never sold user data and will not do so in the future.

11.4 Right to Non-Discrimination

We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights. You will receive the same service quality regardless of your privacy choices.

11.5 Authorized Agent

You can designate an authorized agent to make CCPA requests on your behalf. The agent must provide proof of authorization.

11.6 CCPA Request Process

  1. Email privacy@hypeai.io with "CCPA Request" in the subject line
  2. Include your name, email, and specific request type
  3. We will verify your identity (may request additional information)
  4. We will respond within 45 days (may extend to 90 days if complex)
  5. No charge for up to 2 requests per 12 months

California residents can request information about personal data shared with third parties for marketing purposes (once per year). Contact us at privacy@hypeai.io.
