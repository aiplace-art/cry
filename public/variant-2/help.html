HypeAI is an AI-powered cryptocurrency platform built on Binance Smart Chain. We offer 27 specialized AI agents that help with trading, analysis, portfolio management, and more. Our platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with the speed and affordability of BSC.

Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right corner. Select your wallet provider (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.). Approve the connection request. Your wallet will be connected and you can start using HypeAI.

We support MetaMask, Trust Wallet, WalletConnect, Binance Chain Wallet, and any BSC-compatible wallet.

HypeAI charges a 0.3% trading fee per transaction. BSC gas fees (approximately $0.10) apply to all blockchain transactions.

Stake your HYPE tokens to earn 62% APY rewards. Simply lock your tokens in the staking contract, and rewards are distributed automatically every block. You can unstake at any time after the minimum lock period.

The minimum staking amount is 100 HYPE tokens. There is no maximum limit.

AI agents are specialized algorithms that perform specific tasks like market analysis, trading automation, portfolio optimization, and risk assessment. HypeAI offers 27 different agents, each expert in their domain.

Basic AI agents are free for all users. Premium agents and advanced features require HYPE token holdings or a subscription.

You can buy HYPE tokens through our platform, PancakeSwap, or other DEXs on Binance Smart Chain. Connect your wallet, ensure you have BNB for gas fees, and swap BNB for HYPE.

There is no minimum trade amount, but very small trades may not be economical due to gas fees.
